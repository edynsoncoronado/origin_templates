Routes for our applications, URLs for our application.

The concept is the same one though.

The idea is that we have different paths, which is the part after the domain, so something like:

  • slash nothing
  • slash post
  • slash posts, slash something else.

And that for these different paths we can trigger different actions.

And these actions are our views.

It's the logic that is executed for different URLs.

And we can build them as a function or as a class.

And then there we handle the incoming request and return a response.
